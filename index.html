How do I install mods manually? ›

  1. Download a plugin (.esp) and the associated resources archive (.bsa). Note: Not all mods have or need archives.
  2. Copy the plugin and archive to your Steam\steamapps\common\Skyrim\Data folder.
  3. Enable the plugin with your choice of mod manager.

How to Install

  1. Download a plugin (.esp) and the associated resources archive (.ba2). Note: Not all mods have or need archives.
  2. Copy the plugin and archive to your Steam\steamapps\common\Fallout 4\Data folder.
  3. Enable the plugin from within Fallout 4 or with your choice of mod manager.

Descriptions

Alternate Entrance: Bleak Falls Barrow Go to Top of Page ›

Adds a large rock and switch inside the exit from Bleak Falls Barrow so that you can enter through the backway

Better Favor Jobs - Legendary Go to Top of Page ›

  • Sell ore and ingots in bulk
  • Receive dynamically adjusted gold from bulk transactions
  • Earn XP rewards equivalent to selling ore and ingots one by one

Better Master Trainers - Legendary Go to Top of Page ›

Increases the master training skill to 200 for use with the Legendary Edition

Campfire Cooking - Legendary Go to Top of Page ›

Allows the player to activate campfires to cook meals

Interactive Clutter - Legendary Go to Top of Page ›

Turns more than 11,000 static objects into interactive objects that can be picked up, thrown, and deconstructed

Interactive Cobwebs - Legendary Go to Top of Page ›

Allows the player to remove cobwebs by activating them

Invisible Wall Remover: Sky Haven Temple Go to Top of Page ›

  • Removes the invisible wall surrounding the Sky Haven Temple
  • Allows the player to enter the temple before the respective quest

King of Thieves Go to Top of Page ›

Master of Disguise - Special Edition Go to Top of Page ›

Master of Disguise for Skyrim was featured by GameSpot, Kotaku, Brodual, MxR Mods, and the Skyrim G.E.M.S. Hall of Fame, the #1 Stealth mod on the Steam Workshop and the Skyrim Nexus, and has more than 1 million views on YouTube.

Updated to be compatible with the Skyrim Special Edition. Not compatible with non-SE versions of Skyrim.

Known Issues:

  • Guard disguises are still broken.
  • SkyUI text does not reflect changes to mod distribution.

No Combat Boundaries - Legendary Go to Top of Page ›

  • Removes combat boundaries from encounter zones
  • Enables hostiles to follow the player through doors and between encounter zones

Stay With Me - Legendary Go to Top of Page ›

  • Conflict-free, cloak-based follower leash (i.e., moves distant/slow/lost followers to you faster)
  • Supports all followers, summons, and companions out of the box
  • Based on Followers Keep Up by NorthStar
